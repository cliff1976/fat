Out to IKEA and back via the Ho Chi Minh Trail

April 10, 2022 · cliff1976

It was pretty today. But cold when the sun wasn’t right on me. I headed east out towards IKEA and marveled at the completed work on the A3 — three lanes in each direction — and then headed over to my buddy’s house by way of what he calls the Ho Chi Minh trail down the hill from the overflow parking at the Uni-Klinikum.

The timestamps from the bike cam are off by an hour because I didn’t get around to updating that for DST this year yet. I hope I remember for next time!

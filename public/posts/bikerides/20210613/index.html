Do the big hills first...works much better!

June 13, 2021 · cliff1976

I went up those hills south of Regensburg on the way to Pentling that used to discourage me. I won’t say they’re easy now, but I can chug along in my lowest gear without feeling like I’m risking a heart attack. Basically abandon all hope, ye who enter Kumpfmühl…it’s a long, mostly uphill slog to Hohengebraching. But after that it was pretty nice all the rest of the way back home.

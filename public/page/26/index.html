Saturday Bad Abbach Loop

Another beautiful day — well, at the beginning. By the end of my ride, the clouds started looming and threatening rain, but I got home before that started. And it blew over anyways. I had worried about two things: Headed south out of Regensburg is a more direct route, but getting up those hills and down in to Bad Abbach would be a real pain. The ferry at Matting would be closed due to Corona stuff....
